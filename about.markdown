I'm Aya Belkat, a Space Engineer specialising in GNC and Astrodynamics.

I hold an MSc in Space Engineering with Distinction from the University of Surrey, awarded the International Excellence Postgraduate Scholarship for academic merit. I was selected among 15 African students for the inaugural ESA–AfSA Space Systems Engineering Training Course, and hold a BSc in Mechanical Engineering from Hacettepe University as an honour student, funded by the Türkiye Bursları Scholarship.

My work focuses on trajectory optimisation, mission design, and guidance, navigation and control — most recently designing and optimising a flyby mission to asteroid 2024 YR4.

I write about space exploration — the philosophy behind why we go, and the technical work of getting there. My writing is aimed at anyone curious about space, whether you work in the industry or not.

I'm currently working on projects in astrodynamics and GNC, with all work publicly available on [GitHub](https://github.com/AyaBelkat).

Currently based in Tetouan, Morocco.
